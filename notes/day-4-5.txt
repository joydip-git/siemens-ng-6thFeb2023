Forms in Angilar: 
	Template-driven forms: 
		control everything from angular design
	Reactive forms:
		control everything from code

1. FormsModule: (Template-driven and Reactive Forms)
		directives: ngForm, ngModel etc.
		classes: FormGroup, FormControl etc.

2. ReactiveFormsModule: (Reactive Forms)
		directives: formGroupName, formGroup, formControlName, formControl etc.
